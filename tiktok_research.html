Why TikTok Is an Addictive App and How Does It Affect the User's Mental Health?

By Zannatul Naeem

TikTok is one of the most popular app in the world. Almost all of us have a TikTok app on our phones. It's great for entertainment, passing time and sometimes it's even good for sharing information. As a regular user of the app, one thing that I've noticed is that once I start using the app, sometimes it's hard to get off. There were many times at night where I've used TikTok and the next thing I realized that I've spent hours scrolling through the app. This phenomenon isn't only experienced by me. I have friends who completely ruined their standard sleep schedule ever since they started using TikTok. One question pounders upon my mind: Why is TikTok so addicting? Is it impacting people's mental health without them realizing? I'm hoping to find the answers throughout this research.
